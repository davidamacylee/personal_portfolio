Sound Healing

Sound healing is a therapeutic practice that harnesses the power of sound vibrations to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and enhance overall well-being. Through the intentional use of various instruments such as singing bowls, gongs, and tuning forks, practitioners create a harmonious auditory environment. The resonating frequencies interact with the body, influencing its energetic and physical aspects. Sound healing is believed to facilitate healing at a cellular level, balance energy centers, and induce a meditative state, offering individuals a unique and holistic approach to wellness for the mind, body, and spirit.


I offer 30, 60, and 90 minute Sound Bath sessions which are tailored to the individual's state of being and needs. I also offer group sessions for yoga classes and guided meditations.

Qi Gong

Qi Gong, rooted in traditional Chinese medicine, is a holistic practice that combines intentional movement, breath control, and meditation to cultivate and balance the body's vital life force, known as "Qi" or "Chi." Through gentle flowing movements and focused breathing exercises, practitioners harness the energy within and around them, promoting physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual harmony. Qi Gong serves as a pathway to enhance energy flow, reduce stress, and foster overall well-being.

Yoga

Yoga, originating from ancient Indian traditions, is a holistic practice that unites breath, movement, and mindfulness to promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. Through a combination of postures (asanas), breath control (pranayama), and meditation, yoga seeks to harmonize the mind and body. It offers a diverse range of styles, from the dynamic and energizing to the restorative and contemplative, catering to various preferences and needs. Yoga is not just a physical exercise but a transformative journey that enhances flexibility, strength, and inner awareness, fostering a sense of balance and tranquility in daily life.
